About Me

Riley Kelfer

Up until August of 2025, I was a Northeasterner, through and through. Born and raised in Massachussetts, I got my Bachelor’s degree in Maine, at Colby College, and then my Master’s in Connecticut, at Yale. After living and teaching for a year in noisy Manhattan, I reckoned (in the words of Huck Finn) "I got to light out for the Territory."

Now, I’m happily living in the Mueller neighborhood of Austin, Texas. I come to tutoring from classroom teaching, though in fact, working as a tutor preceded my first-ever teaching job, when I fellowed for a film history course on Hollywood Cinema. Prior to that fellowship, I’d worked at Colby's on-campus Writing Center, eventually serving as Head Tutor, and later at Yale's Poorvu Center for Teaching and Learning.

My tutoring practice is much informed by my being trained in eclectic educational contexts, including a wide range of grade levels, student profiles, and classroom settings. On top of (and owing to) this diverse experience, I aim to bring patience, tact, and adaptability to every educational engagement. For the past six years, my ultimate goal as a tutor has been to help all kinds of students find joy and self-expression in learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects do you tutor?

I specialize in English topics from elementary school to college; however, I also frequently support students with Math topics through Pre-algebra, as well as any other core subjects like History and Science.

Where do sessions take place?

Most families prefer to host tutoring sessions at their home. When hosting is not preferable for a family, I offer use of a dedicated office space (weekends only) in Mueller, or a nearby public library. Certain schools in the area allow me to tutor on their premises, on account of my holding active tutoring insurance.

What is your cancellation policy?

I require 24 hours' notice for cancellations to avoid a session fee.

How do you calculate your rates?

It depends on the service(s) requested, as my associated prep time can vary greatly. For a given service, I start with a base rate and apply a standard formula for travel time. I strive to make sure the cost of tutoring does not strain any family situation.

What is your pay structure?

I am flexible in regard to families' preference for payment cadence within a given block of sessions. Some families prefer to pay ahead for a set number of sessions. A detailed breakdown of pay structure can be found in each specific tutoring contract, which I prepare following our first consultation.

Do you tutor remotely?

Yes! Depending on the situation and family needs, I conduct sessions via Zoom for students both in and outside of Austin.
